Our story

Christopher and Josefin are our names. We met eachother in the philipines when we both worked in the same dog shelter and helped find new homes for stray dogs. Our love for dogs have always been there, and that's what brought us together to start this daycare, Pawradise! It was not easy to find the perfect house for our dreams to come true. We wanted a big enough yard for a lot of dogs to run free and close enough to big parks to walk. Finally we managed to find this big house in Solna, Stockholm! With help from friends and family we renovated the house to fit a lot of dogs. In October 2025 we could finally open the doors and welcome new fur friends to our dog daycare! We are located here

Our vision

Our vision is to always feel that your dog is in good hands when you need to leave your beloved dog. Whether you are on work or on a holiday! We know that feeling when you are on a vacation and every dog that you see on the streets reminds you of your own dog. That's were Pawradise comes in! Our dog daycare is not your usual one. We want your dog to feel like it's on holiday too! Here you can read more about or services
