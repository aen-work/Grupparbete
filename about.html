Our story

We're Christofer and Josefin, the owners of Pawradise. Our journey began with a shared dream: to create a space where dogs feel safe, happy, and truly at home. With backgrounds in animal care and small business management (and a house full of rescue pups), we combined our passions to launch this daycare from the ground up. Every tail wag, playful bark, and sleepy cuddle reminds us why we started this adventure. We believe in personalized care, positive reinforcement, and treating every dog like family. Whether it's a high-energy playgroup or a quiet afternoon with a senior pup, we tailor each experience to meet the needs of our fur friends. Our mission is simple: happy dogs, happy humans. And we're so grateful to be part of your dog's story.

Our vision

Our vision is to always feel that your dog is in good hands when you need to leave your beloved dog. Whether you are on work or on a holiday! We know that feeling when you are on a vacation and every dog that you see on the streets reminds you of your own dog waiting for you at home. That's were Pawradise comes in! Our dog daycare is not your usual one. We want your dog to feel like it's on holiday too!
